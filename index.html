Voting Resources

Voting Requirements

  • Be a U.S. citizen
  • Be over 18 years of age and have valid ID
  • Not a felon
  • Must meet your state's residency requirements

Ballot Drop Off Locations

  • Corner of Schofield and Denver in Kenton
  • Corner of Sandy and 42nd in Hollywood
  • Corner of MLK and Russell in Lloyd

Voting Process

  1. Meet registration requirements
  2. Click here to register
  3. Receive ballot and fill out
  4. Drop off ballot at one of the official ballot boxes
